Pool Shark Games

Pool Shark Games is a personal game studio branding design project. For this project I picked brand colors, created a logo and style guide, as well as wine labels and other promotional materials.


Unifying the approach

During the brainstorming phase, I played around with both the idea of a literal shark in a swimming pool, as well as an expert pool player. I created a variety of shapes and silhouettes of sharks, pool balls and cues, and water themed textures. This lead to multiple concepts for each approach, but the more I experimented, the more i focused in on the idea of trying to combine the two concepts into a unique design.

After my exploration, I experimented with several ways to combine the two concepts. As I experimented, I kept coming back to the imagery of sharks circling around an eight ball. I came up with a handful of variations on this concept which helped refine the silhouettes and shapes used in the final design.

Finding the right typeface

Once I had settled on the concept of a shark in a swimming pool being integrated into the design, I wanted to use a font that felt reminiscent of branding that you might see on pool toys or beach chairs. Sans Serifs had the sort of casual/playful look that I was looking for, which ultimately helped lead me to the final font choice of Sharik Sans.

Standard logo presentation

This will be the primary logo format when possible. This version displays well on title screens, social media, and merchandise.
