Lost ARK Design

This project was a personal logo design for hypothetical freelance graphic design business. The name Lost ARK Design comes from my initials (A.R.K). I wanted to use the imagery associated with the Ark of the Covenant to create a unique and striking logo.


Digging up inspiration

During the brainstorming phase, I looked to biblical references and imagery for the Ark of the Covenant. The element of the Ark's description that kept standing out to me was the two cherubim (angels), that ornamented the lid. The angel wings seemed like the most interesting element to focus on for this logo mark.

After my exploration, I began to experiment with the idea of the two facing angels. I tried several ideas involving the angels facing and circling each other. I tried to capture the feeling of the two angels kneeling towards over the ark in between them as if to protect or bless it. In the end, I chose to represent this by having two wings circling and wrapping around a central point as seen in the final logo mark.

Finding an elegant typeface

This brand name conveys a sense of gravitas and elegance, which should be reflected in the typeface. I decided to accomplish this by paring the logo mark with a bold serif font. While experimenting with different options, it became clear that a thicker serif font would stand up to and match the weight of the logo mark best. Bell MT is a beautiful serif font that is efficient in both text and display environments due to its relative thickness when compared to other serif fonts.
